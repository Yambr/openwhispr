<objective>
Under `PROVIDER_LOCKDOWN_ENABLED`, reduce the inference-mode selector to exactly two
modes — `openwhispr` (Cloud) and `local` — by DCE-eliminating the `providers`,
`self-hosted`, and `enterprise` entries from the `modes` array in
`InferenceConfigEditor.tsx` and gating their corresponding mount branches. Also
strip the alternative-cloud-provider choice and per-provider API-key inputs from
`ReasoningModelSelector.tsx`.

Purpose: PLD-04. The reasoning UI is NOT a simple Cloud/Local toggle — it is a
5-entry `modes` array (`openwhispr`, `providers`, `local`, `self-hosted`,
`enterprise`) fed to `<InferenceModeSelector>`. The corporate build must present
only `openwhispr` and `local`. Reasoning Cloud mode talks only to our server;
centralized AI provisioning lives server-side. The client must not carry
enterprise-credential UI, a self-hosted BYOK endpoint panel, or a user-facing
reasoning-provider choice.

Output:
- `modes` array in `InferenceConfigEditor.tsx` built so that under lockdown it
  contains ONLY `openwhispr` and `local`; the `providers`, `self-hosted`, and
  `enterprise` entries are dropped via a literal-foldable construction Rolldown DCEs.
- The three lockdown-removed mount branches gated:
  `{config.mode === "providers" && renderModelSelector("cloud")}`,
  `{config.mode === "self-hosted" && <OpenAICompatiblePanel/>}`,
  `{config.mode === "enterprise" && <EnterpriseSection/>}`.
- Cloud reasoning provider selector + per-provider `ApiKeyInput` blocks in
  `ReasoningModelSelector.tsx` gated.
- `EnterpriseSection` and `OpenAICompatiblePanel` subtrees DCE'd under lockdown.
</objective>
